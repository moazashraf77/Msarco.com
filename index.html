About

Masar Al-Mokhtas Contracting Company was established in 2008 AD continued to implement many projects under the name of an institution until 2023

Msar SPECIALIST Contracting Company was established in 2008 and continued to implement many projects under the name of an institution until 2023, when the current name was finalized and it became a company, praise be to God, with the same management, engineers, supervisors, and workers. If any change occurred, it was for the better. We work on executing government and private contracting projects of all kinds, whether commercial, residential, recreational, or others, using specialized engineers, supervisors, and trained, skilled labor, and employing the best modern techniques and methods to provide the best living standards for the modern person in the place. We specialize in designing, implementing, supervising, and following up on all interior and exterior decoration and furnishing works for buildings. We conduct studies and designs to achieve the best results, suggestions, and solutions that suit the client’s needs for the intended use of the place. We undertake the final stages of buildings and projects, carrying out all works related to project completion, including finishing, interior and exterior decorations, and providing design plans, execution plans, and samples required by owners and consulting offices at a high standard, before starting the implementation of works according to the required schedules for each phase of the project.
